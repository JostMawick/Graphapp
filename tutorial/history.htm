History

Origins of GraphApp:

  • Based loosely on Libg by Rob Pike (the Plan 9 graphics library)
  • Ported to Macintosh by Loki (me) in 1992
  • Ported to Windows and expanded by Loki in 1993
  • Ported back to X-Windows in 1995
  • Now works with Windows, X-Windows (and Macintosh).

Modern Influences:

  • LibSX
  • Java's AWT system
